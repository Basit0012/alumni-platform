# 🎓 Alumni Interaction & Networking Platform - User Guide

Welcome to the Alumni Platform! This guide will walk you through how to use every feature of the application, depending on whether you are a **Student**, an **Alumni**, or an **Admin**.

---

## 👤 1. Getting Started (Authentication)
### Registration
* Go to `/register`.
* Fill in your Name, Email, and Password.
* **Important:** Select your role (`Student` or `Alumni`) from the dropdown. This determines what features you can access.

### Login
* Go to `/login` and enter your credentials.
* Upon login, you will be automatically redirected to your personalized **Feed**.

---

## 📰 2. The Community Feed (`/feed`)
The feed is the central hub for discussions, achievements, and updates.
* **View Posts:** Scroll through the timeline to see what others are sharing.
* **Create a Post:** Use the text box at the top to share an update, a job opportunity, or ask a question.
* **Interact:** You can **Like** posts to show support and **Comment** on posts to start a discussion.
* **Manage:** You can delete your own posts at any time.

---

## 🔍 3. Alumni Directory (`/alumni`)
This is where networking happens. You can browse through the verified alumni of your institution.
* **Search & Filter:** Use the search bar to find alumni by name, or use the dropdown to filter by their **Batch Year**.
* **View Profiles:** Click on an alumni's name to view their full profile, including their current Company, Designation, and Bio.
* **Connect:** Click the **Connect** button on an alumni's card to send them a networking request (similar to LinkedIn).

---

## 🤝 4. Connections & Networking
Once you send a connection request, the other person receives it.
* **Accept/Reject:** If someone sends you a request, you can either **Accept** or **Reject** it.
* **Connected:** Once accepted, you form a formal network link with that user.

---

## 💡 5. Mentorship Program
This is a premium feature allowing Students to learn directly from experienced Alumni.
* **Request Mentorship (Student):** When viewing an Alumni in the directory, students will see a special **"Request Mentorship"** button. Click it to ask them for career guidance.
* **Approve Mentorship (Alumni):** Alumni will receive mentorship requests from students. They can review the student's profile and choose to **Approve** or **Reject** the request based on their availability.

---

## 📅 6. Events & Meetups (`/events`)
Stay updated on workshops, reunions, and webinars.
* **Browse Events:** View the grid of upcoming events. You'll see the date, time, location, and the organizer.
* **Online vs Physical:** Events marked with a pulsing green **"Online"** badge are virtual.
* **Register:** Click **"Register for Event"** to secure your seat.
* **Join Meeting:** If you registered for an online event, a **"Join Meeting"** button will appear, linking you directly to the Zoom/Google Meet.
* **Host an Event (Alumni/Admin only):** If you are an Alumni or Admin, a "Host a New Event" form will appear at the top. Fill in the details (Title, Date, Location, Meeting Link) to invite the community.

---

## ⚙️ 7. Admin Privileges
If you are logged in as an **Administrator**, you have platform-wide control.
* **Dashboard (`/admin/dashboard`):** View total statistics (Total Users, Posts, Events, Mentorships).
* **Moderation:** Admins have the ability to delete inappropriate posts or events.
* **User Management:** Admins can oversee user roles and activity.

---

### 📝 Quick Tips for Success
* **Complete your Profile:** Go to `/profile` and fill out your bio, company, and designation. A complete profile gets 3x more connection requests!
* **Be Active:** Post your projects, ask questions in the feed, and don't hesitate to request mentorship from alumni working at your dream company.
